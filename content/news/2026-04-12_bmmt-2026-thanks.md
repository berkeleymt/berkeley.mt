To all coaches, students, and parents,

What an incredible day! Thank you so much for joining us at BmMT 2026. From the
Puzzle Round in the morning to the Awards Ceremony in the evening, the energy on
campus was electric, and that's entirely because of you.

friendships, fun memories, and maybe even a new favorite math problem.

A huge thank you also goes out to our volunteers, sponsors, and the coaches and
parents who made the trek to Berkeley — none of this would be possible without
your support.
