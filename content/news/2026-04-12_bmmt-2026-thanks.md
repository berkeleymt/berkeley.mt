To all coaches, students, and parents,

What an incredible day! Thank you so much for joining us at BmMT 2026. From the
Puzzle Round in the morning to the Awards Ceremony in the evening, the energy on
campus was electric, and that's entirely because of you.

<!-- more -->

We loved watching students puzzle through creative challenges, strategize during
Relay, and cheer each other on throughout the day. Whether it was your first
math competition or your fifth BmMT, we hope you walked away with new
friendships, fun memories, and maybe even a new favorite math problem.

A huge thank you also goes out to our volunteers, sponsors, and the coaches and
parents who made the trek to Berkeley — none of this would be possible without
your support.

We're always looking to improve, so if you have a few minutes, we'd love to
hear what you thought! Please fill out our feedback survey below.

{% button_link(href="https://tinyurl.com/bmmt2026survey") %} BmMT 2026
Experience Survey {% end %}

Score reports will be sent out soon. In the meantime, we hope you'll carry the
excitement from today forward — and we can't wait to see you again next year!

Sincerely,\
Your friends @ the Berkeley Math Tournament
