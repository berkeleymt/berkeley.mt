<h2>Teardown Timeline</h2>

<h3>End of Relay (~3:30 PM)</h3>

<ul>
<li><strong>Teardown manager:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Send out announcement for Teardown volunteers to meet outside Wheeler</li>
<li>Bring all our tubes of wipes (except 1 maybe)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>After releasing students, proctors should:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Rearrange desks</li>
<li>Gather large pieces of trash</li>
<li>Head to their next assigned location (Activities, Teardown)</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>During Activities (~3:40 PM)</h3>

<ul>
<li>Split off into groups of 2–3</li>
<li><strong>Order:</strong> WHLR → VLSB → DWIN non-activities' classrooms</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Room Checklist</strong> (do this in every room/auditorium!):</p>
<ul>
<li>Pick up trash from floors and desks. If students have drawn on the desks, try to erase as much as you can.</li>
<li>Reorganize desks and chairs to proper order</li>
<li>Ensure the projector has been turned off</li>
<li>Wipe down desks</li>
<li>Send a picture in the Discord thread (<strong>#Teardown Photos</strong>) with the message <strong>[BUILDING, ROOM #]</strong></li>
<li>Take off BMT sign from door</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>In Hallways:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Take off any BMT signage
<ul>
<li>If paper, throw away (recycle)</li>
<li>If not, bring back to Dwinelle Ops HQ</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Pick up any trash</li>
<li>Leave up any signage related to Awards / auditoriums</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>In Satellite Ops HQs:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Recycle any papers, toss away food waste, etc.</li>
<li>Rearrange chairs and desks, and make sure there are the right number of each</li>
<li>Bring back any testing boxes, blank scratch paper reams, leftover untouched food, people's items if they aren't there, etc. to DWIN 211</li>
<li>Send picture to Discord thread</li>
</ul>

<h3>End of Activities / During Awards (~5:00 PM)</h3>

<ul>
<li>Head to Dwinelle Activities rooms
<ul>
<li>Materials should have been brought back to DWIN 211 by activities hosts</li>
<li>Rooms: DWIN 182, 183, 187, 189, 109, 106, 105, 246, 247, 258</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Same room checklist as above</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>In Hallways:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Start taking down signage</li>
<li>Bring sandwich boards into DWIN 211 (Ops HQ), recycle paper signage</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>In DWIN 211 (Ops HQ):</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Throw away all food boxes and food waste</li>
<li>Use the Inventory Manager's list to understand what to keep and throw out</li>
</ul>

<h3>End of Awards</h3>

<ul>
<li><strong>Teardown manager:</strong> Send out announcement for each auditorium to begin teardown & the group photo will be taken ~30 minutes after end of awards, so the auditorium needs to be entirely cleaned up by then</li>
<li><strong>For each auditorium:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Drop off any BMT tables, tablecloth, any spare awards to DWIN 211 before coming to Pauley</li>
<li>Recycle signage from auditorium doors</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>After Group Photo</h3>

<p><strong>Teardown Pauley:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Clean up trash</li>
<li>Rearrange chairs</li>
<li>Unplug and return mics</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Teardown Ops Corridor:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Assign people to smaller parts of the Ops Corridor</li>
<li><strong>Recycle:</strong> Used scratch paper</li>
<li><strong>Keep:</strong> Test boxes, envelopes, materials</li>
<li>Shove everything into the UHaul</li>
</ul>
